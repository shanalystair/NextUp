About NEXTUP Queue System

Revolutionizing the queuing experience at Emilio Aguinaldo College - Cavite campus with smart, efficient, and user-friendly queue management.

What is NEXTUP?

NEXTUP is an innovative digital queue management system designed specifically for Emilio Aguinaldo College - Cavite. It eliminates long waiting times, reduces physical congestion, and provides a seamless experience for students, faculty, and staff.

Our system allows users to join virtual queues from anywhere, receive real-time updates on their position, and get notified when it's their turn to be served.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a stress-free, efficient, and modern queuing solution that enhances the campus experience for every member of the EAC-C community.

Vision: To be the leading digital queue management system in educational institutions, setting the standard for smart campus solutions.

Key Features

  • Real-time Queue Updates: See your exact position in line and estimated wait time
  • Multi-service Support: Queue for Cashier, Registrar, Guidance, Library, Clinic, and IT Office
  • Mobile-Friendly: Access the queue system from any device
  • Smart Notifications: Get alerted when your turn is approaching
  • Admin Dashboard: Comprehensive tools for service staff to manage queues efficiently
  • Secure Authentication: Protected student accounts with encrypted data
  • Analytics & Reports: Track queue performance and service efficiency
  • Zero Physical Contact: Reduce crowding and maintain social distancing
